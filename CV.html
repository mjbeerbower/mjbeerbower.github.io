News

The Coalition for Responsible Home Education named April 30th the Day of the Home Schooled Child. As a home school alumunus, I support the work of CRHE. Math is one of the most empowering skills for individuals in every profession. I believe that every child has the right to access qualified, effective, and communal mathematics education and experiences. Understanding the complexity of academic achievement in home education is vital to forming just public policy and to providing academic and career support to home school alumni.
