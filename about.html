About

I am a graduate student and teaching assistant at University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee. My current research focuses on Fubini rankings. I love combinatorics. So far, my experience is with algebraic combinatorics, but I am branching out into analytic combinatorics.

Presenting poster at the Great Plains Combinatorics Conference at University of Kansas, May 2026.

Counting ℓ-Fubini rankings through their outcomes group: Bjorn Ager-Hart, Joakim Jakovleski, Melissa Beerbower, Dr. Pamela E. Harris, and Matthew McClinton, November 2025.
